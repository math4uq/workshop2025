About the Workshop

Join leading researchers and practitioners for two days of talks and discussions at RWTH Aachen, focusing on the latest methods in stochastic modeling, uncertainty quantification, and their applications in engineering and data science. A Zoom link will be provided to all registered participants. Attendance in person is limited to invited guests.
