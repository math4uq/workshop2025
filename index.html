About the Workshop

Join leading researchers and practitioners for two days of talks and discussions at RWTH Aachen, focusing on the latest methods in stochastic modeling, uncertainty quantification, and their applications in engineering and data science. A Zoom link will be provided to all registered participants. Attendance in person is limited to invited guests.

Confirmed speakers include:
  • Abdul-Lateef Haji-Ali — Heriot-Watt University
  • Ana Djurdjevac — Freie Universität Berlin
  • Christian Bayer — WIAS Berlin
  • Fabio Nobile — EPFL
  • Gerardo Rubino — Inria, Univ. Rennes, IRISA
  • Håkon Hoel — University of Oslo
  • Jonas Kiessling — Qubos Systematic / Black Ark Capital
  • Lars Grasedyck — RWTH Aachen
  • Luis Espath — University of Nottingham
  • Martin Eigel — WIAS Berlin
  • Philipp Knechtges — DLR
